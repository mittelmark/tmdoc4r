Introduction

This is a Markdown file with a header inclusion on top and a demonstration on how to embed LaTeX equations using the CodeCogs web service. The equations are embedded like this:

'``{.mtex}
E = mc^2  \tag{1}
'``

Here the output:

E = mc^2  \tag{1}

And here as an example a matrix displayed:

'``{.mtex}
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\      \tag{2}
4 & 5 & 6 
\end{bmatrix}
'``

And here the output:

\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\      \tag{2}
4 & 5 & 6 
\end{bmatrix}

Again using the option echo=false we can as well hide the code to generate the equation or the matrix like so:

'``{.mtex echo=false}
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\      \tag{3}
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
'``
